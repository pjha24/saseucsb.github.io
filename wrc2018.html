About SASE Regionals

The 2018 SASE West Regional Conference is an event which invites company speakers to host professional development workshops and provides networking opportunities to members across the West Region.

Eighteen active chapters in the West Region (including the University of Southern California, UC Berkeley, Stanford University, and UC San Diego) will travel to the conference to gain insight from experienced individuals about topics which range from introduction to industry to refining lifelong career skills. Located in the greater Santa Barbara area, the West Regional Conference offers beautiful scenic views, surrounded by ocean and mountain ranges, as well as a friendly, competitive environment built on a supportive community.

The 2018 West Regional Conference theme — "Empowering You to Act" — aims to inspire students to motivate themselves and continue succeeding to their maximum potential. The event will not only prepare students for success for their careers, but also provide a hand to the community through local charities and community service.
